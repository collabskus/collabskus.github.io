# Hello World

Welcome to the CollabsKus blog!

We are a small team based in Kathmandu, Nepal, building open-source tools and sharing ideas with the world. This blog is where we write about what we are working on, what we are learning, and what inspires us.

## Why CollabsKus?

CollabsKus started as a simple project: a live Kathmandu calendar and time display. Over time, it grew into a platform where we explore the intersection of technology and Nepali culture.

Our goal is to make Nepali calendars, timekeeping, and astronomical data accessible to everyone through clean, open-source software.
